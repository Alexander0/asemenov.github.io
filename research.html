Research Overview

My expertise is in machine learning, optimization, network analysis, and the analysis of very large datasets. I focus on developing efficient algorithms and data-driven models to address complex interdisciplinary problems across domains such as social networks, healthcare, metabolomics, and 3D printing digital twins.
